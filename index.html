    Whats New

  • Sarvodaya Vidyalaya School Bus

    Eighteen School buses run to different parts of the city. Information regarding bus routes and stops can be got from the school office. The students can travel by the school bus only after remitting the bus fees for the whole year, and obtaining the Journey Card from the School office. However it can be paid in three instalments on the due dates, failing which a fine of Rs. 20/- for each month will have to be paid.
